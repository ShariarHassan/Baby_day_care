Time & Date of Services

     	Admission process:
 Admission is open for all kids
 Age Level for Pre-School and Day Care admission:
Pre-School: 2 Years for ECD-I and 3-5 Years  for ECD-II
Day Care: 6 months to 6 years
    * Kids admitted for the Day Care service will also get Pre-School service appropriate for their age level.
 Getting admission depends on the availability of seat
 ‘First come first serve’ policy followed for admitting kids
Required documents for admission:
 Filled-up admission form
 Filled-up emergency form
 Recent Photograph of Child, Mother and Father or Legal guardian in absence of parents
(Two copies of each)
 Photocopy of the Birth Certificate of the child
 Photocopy of National ID card of Mother and Father or Legal guardian in absence of
parents
