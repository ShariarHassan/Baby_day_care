Services



In ChildCheer kids can stay in a familiar environment under the care of a professional caregiver with relevant qualifications and skills. It provides the opportunity for both parents to pursue careers without having to sacrifice or compromise their professional goals. It has the advantage of having a regular schedule, which creates stability for a child and his/her parents. It creates a nurturing environment for kids.





The demand of institutional care for babies is increasing at an alarming rate because the socialization of children is deteriorating. Under the circumstances, babies are socializing through peer group interaction in ChildCheer. They learn important skills such as sharing and making friends.





Children's brains develop quickly for that reason they require age appropriate activities which stimulate development, nutrition and protection otherwise growth will be delayed. Children learn mostly by observing and interacting with objects in their environment mainly through exploring, manipulating, constructing and playing with different materials. In ChildCheer kids have the time in which they play and take part in cultural life such as reading, storytelling, music, painting and carving.





ChildCheer is a platform of early care, stimulation and learning. The services of ChildCheer combine childcare with pedagogical work in an activity that takes daylong responsibility for the whole child. It has been observed that the performance of babies is good in schools who attended in the Early Childhood Development center or pre-school. In ChildCheer, kids grow and learn comfortably with other children and adults.
